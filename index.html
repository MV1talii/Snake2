"Snake" game

Очки: 0

Instructions

  • Objective: The objective of the game is to control a snake on the screen and help it eat as much food as possible. As the snake consumes food, it grows longer, and the player's score increases.

  • Controls: Use the arrow keys on your keyboard (up, down, left, right)to control the direction of the snake's movement. Be cautious and avoid running into the walls or the snake's body, as doing so will end the game.

  • Scoring: Your score increases with each food item the snake eats. Generally, the more food the snake consumes, the higher the score.
